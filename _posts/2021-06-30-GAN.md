We have come across various articles and posts about AI being capable of producing human like speech or generating images of non-existing people that are difficult to distinguish from real-life existence. These AI systems are built upon generative adversarial networks (GANs) - which Facebook AI research director Yann LeCun called "most interesting idea in last 10 years in ML"
